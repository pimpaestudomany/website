Park Upgrade Update

By Tom Fletcher · 5 July 2026

Council crews broke ground on the Riverside Park upgrade this week, with a new playground, shaded seating, and a looped walking track expected to open in spring.

The park will remain open throughout construction, though the northern entrance will be closed for safety.
